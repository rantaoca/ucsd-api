Computer Science & Engineering


CSE CONTACT: CSE Advisors at csepeeradviser@eng.ucsd.edu, CSE Building Room 1231.
Check your @ucsd.edu email for course and enrollment announcements. Exceptions will not be made for students who fail to receive or read their UCSD email.
For CSE Tentative Course Offerings: http://cse.ucsd.edu/node/42 4 http://cse.ucsd.edu/
PREREQUISITES ARE ENFORCED IN ALL COURSES: For additional prerequisite information, visit the Courses Pages for Undergraduate Education or Graduate Courses: http://cse.ucsd.edu/node/42
CSE COURSE CLEARANCE REQUEST: Please email csepeeradviser@eng.ucsd.edu, with your name, PID number, course number and title, and reason for your request.
ADDING COURSES: The WebReg system is used to add a class by the add deadline. NOTE: Some faculty will announce that they will close their class before the add deadline. For more information on how to add a class visit: http://students.ucsd.edu/academics/enroll/ undergraduate-enrollment/add-a-class.html
WAITLIST: If a class is full, please add yourself to the wait list. Wait lists are managed by first come, first serve by the Webreg system. For more information on CSE Course Enrollment Updates visit: http://cse.ucsd.edu/node/2527
